BLACKPINK is a globally renowned South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment, consisting of Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. Known for their powerful music, striking visuals, and innovative performances, BLACKPINK has become one of the most influential K-pop acts worldwide. Beyond their musical impact, the members also use their platform to support various social causes, including climate change. They have partnered with organizations like Climate Action Now and raised awareness on environmental issues. Through their influential presence in fashion and media, BLACKPINK encourages sustainability, promoting eco-friendly fashion choices and advocating for a more conscious approach to consumption. They represent a blend of talent and social responsibility, using their voice to inspire positive change for the environment.
